To join Rose Fish:

Make a contribution that requires human effort to create and could be easily used by the fish community members.

Acceptable Contributions:
  • Public VRChat avatar/world
  • 3D model
  • Script, Code, Utility, Tool
  • Shaders
  • Videos/Animations
  • Music, Sound Effects
  • Drawings
  • Other ＜＞＜-related content

If you believe you have made a significant contribution, you can submit it on the ＜＞＜ discord server at discord.gg/Z34mSSM

🌹 ROSE FISH VOTE? 🌹

After submitting a contribution it will be reviewed by @theziver and decided If a vote is necessary, if so all the rose fish members will participate in a 2-day long voting process. To receive a rose fish invitation‚ the contribution must receive a YES vote from over 60％ of the voting rose fish members․

Deciding if a vote is necessary @THEZIVER will consider these things:

  • Fun: Is it fun?
  • Presentability: Is the contribution well presented and easy to understand?
  • Effort: How much human effort was needed to make this? How much did AI help?
  • Usability: Can it be used for something?
  • Originality: How different is the contribution from previous contributions?
  • Collaboration: How many people were involved in creating the contribution?
